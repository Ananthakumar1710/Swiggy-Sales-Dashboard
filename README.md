Swiggy Delivery & Sales Analysis Dashboard
Project Objective
The goal of this Excel-based project was to analyze Swiggy’s operational data to understand food delivery patterns, restaurant performance, and customer preferences. The dashboard serves as a strategic tool to identify which cities and cuisines drive the highest engagement and where service improvements are needed.

Key Features
City-Wise Performance: Analyzed order volumes across different urban centers to identify market leaders.

Cuisine & Category Insights: Evaluated the popularity of various food categories to help vendors optimize their menus.

Rating & Quality Analysis: Correlated restaurant ratings with order frequency to determine the impact of service quality on sales.

Pricing Strategy: Analyzed cost-per-order trends to understand consumer spending habits across different regions.

Technical Skills Applied
Data Cleaning: Used Power Query to handle raw Swiggy datasets, managing missing values and standardizing city names.

Data Modeling: Created specialized Pivot Tables to aggregate complex delivery metrics.

Interactive Visuals: Designed a dynamic dashboard using Slicers, Timeline filters, and Advanced Charting (Bar, Pie, and Map-style charts).

Lookup Functions: Implemented VLOOKUP/XLOOKUP and Index-Match for seamless data mapping between restaurant IDs and sales records.
